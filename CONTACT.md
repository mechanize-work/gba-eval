## DMCA / Copyright Takedown Notices

If you believe content in this repository infringes a copyright you own
or are authorized to act for, send a notice to the address above that
includes:

1. A physical or electronic signature of the rights holder or an
   authorized agent.
2. Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed.
3. Identification of the material to be removed (URL, file path, commit
   SHA — whatever lets us locate it unambiguously).
4. Your contact information: name, mailing address, telephone number,
   and email address.
5. A statement that you have a good-faith belief that the use is not
   authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
6. A statement, made under penalty of perjury, that the information in
   the notice is accurate and that you are the rights holder or
   authorized to act on its behalf.

Designated agent for receiving DMCA notices: **Stephen Yang, Mechanize, Inc.**,
reachable at the email above.

## Trademark Concerns

If you believe a trademark you own is being misused here — beyond the
nominative descriptive use already documented in
[`LEGAL.md`](LEGAL.md) §3 — please email us with:

1. The mark at issue and your standing to enforce it.
2. The specific use you object to (file path, line, commit).
3. The remedy you are requesting.

## ROM Removal Requests

This repository ships only ROMs the authors have permitted to be
redistributed (see [`corpus/README.md`](corpus/README.md) and
[`LEGAL.md`](LEGAL.md) §2). If you are the author of a ROM included
under `corpus/roms/` and want it removed, email us — we will remove it
on request, without requiring formal process.

## License or Provenance Questions

Questions about license interpretation, the BIOS stub's clean-room

## Counter-Notices

If material you posted has been removed in response to a notice and you
believe the removal was a mistake or misidentification, you may submit
a counter-notice to the same email address with the elements specified
in 17 U.S.C. § 512(g)(3).
